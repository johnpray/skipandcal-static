    Cheat-O-Scope 3000
    October 17, 2007 (published) / May 11, 2012 (posted here)

    This "cartoon" was actually an illustration for a feature on cheating in the Cardinal Courier. It was central in a large two-page spread, though the descriptive sign was omitted in the original printing.

    If you're curious, it turned out that the vast majority of Fisher students don't cheat (at least according to the anonymous survey results).

    This work originally appeared in the Cardinal Courier, the award-winning student-run newspaper at St. John Fisher College, between 2007 and 2011.

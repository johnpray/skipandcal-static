    One Low Price!
    September 17, 2008 (published) / May 16, 2012 (posted here)

    When Fisher’s new dining services first started, its prices at “Late Nite”, as it was called, were a bit astronomical. They got the hint pretty quickly, but not before I drew this little number. (Somewhere in my fantasies I like to imagine this cartoon helped influence that change.)

    This work originally appeared in the Cardinal Courier, the award-winning student-run newspaper at St. John Fisher College, between 2007 and 2011.

    You'd Think They Would Ask
    April 23, 2008 (published) / May 15, 2012 (posted here)

    This appeared shortly after Fisher's administration announced that the company handling the school's dining services would be changing the next semester... after little-to-no input about the decision from the students who actually have to pay for and eat the food.

    This work originally appeared in the Cardinal Courier, the award-winning student-run newspaper at St. John Fisher College, between 2007 and 2011.

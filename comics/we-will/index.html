    We Will
    April 19, 2011 (published) / October 12, 2014 (posted here)

    This strip was Skip and Cal's final one in the Cardinal Courier. It accurately represents my own feelings at graduation.

    But is it their final strip ever? We shall see...

    This work originally appeared in the Cardinal Courier, the award-winning student-run newspaper at St. John Fisher College, between 2007 and 2011.

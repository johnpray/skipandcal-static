    Sniper
    July 10, 2010 (published) / July 25, 2012 (posted here)

    Today on Unlikely Crossovers™, Dora and Boots use their usual anti-Swiper tactic to successfully protect Team Fortress 2's Medic from the Sniper.

    I drew/crafted this rare fan comic during the summer between my junior and senior years. I meant to make more, and still do. My unnatural love for crossovers needs an outlet of some kind.

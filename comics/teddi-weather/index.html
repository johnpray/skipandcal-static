    Teddi Weather
    February 23, 2011 (published) / April 5, 2013 (posted here)

    As if we haven't already thoroughly trampled this ground already.

    I believe this was my second comic (here's the first) covering the Teddi Dance for Love, which was a big event on our small campus.

    The building depicted here is the Student Life Center on Fisher campus, in the large gym of which Teddi is held.

    This work originally appeared in the Cardinal Courier, the award-winning student-run newspaper at St. John Fisher College, between 2007 and 2011.

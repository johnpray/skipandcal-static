    2008 Presidential Candidate Caricatures
    January 30, 2008 (published) / May 12, 2012 (posted here)

    In alphabetical order: Michael Bloomberg, Hillary Clinton, John Edwards, Rudy Giuliana, Al Gore, Mike Huckabee, John McCain, Barack Obama, and Mitt Romney.

    I drew these for the Courier's special on the upcoming presidential election. I think McCain is my favorite. The Obama and McCain ones were re-used later in the year once the race was narrowed down to the two of them.

    This work originally appeared in the Cardinal Courier, the award-winning student-run newspaper at St. John Fisher College, between 2007 and 2011.
